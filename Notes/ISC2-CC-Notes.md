# ISC2 Certified in Cybersecurity (CC) - Technical Notes

This certification covers the fundamental principles of information security required for entry-level cybersecurity professionals.

## Core Domains and Key Concepts:

### 1. Security Principles
* CIA Triad: Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability.
* IAAA: Identification, Authentication, Authorization, and Accountability.
* Governance and Risk Management: Understanding policies, procedures, and risk assessment.

### 2. Business Continuity (BC) and Disaster Recovery (DR)
* Incident Response (IR) Life Cycle.
* Difference between BC (maintaining operations) and DR (restoring systems).

### 3. Access Controls Concepts
* Physical and Logical Access Controls.
* Principle of Least Privilege and Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).

### 4. Network Security
* Understanding the OSI Model and TCP/IP stack.
* Security devices: Firewalls, IDS/IPS, and SIEM.

### 5. Security Operations
* Data Security: Encryption (At rest, In transit, In use).
* Configuration Management and System Hardening.
